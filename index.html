ABOUT US

One craves change, for a variety, for a diversion from the books, classroom lectures, homework and examinations. Amrita School of Engineering Bangalore presents the divergence that all students have been waiting for, the Amrita Kalanjali ‘22. A fest to celebrate and explore the fusion of culture and technology.The event provides a platform for various activities, and the students to exhibit their amazing skills. This year’s Kalanjali digs into the vibrance of Southern Asia, specifically, India, Korea, Japan, Cambodia, Vietnam ,and Thailand.

Sanskriti: The Cultural Wing

Sanskriti, the cultural wing, focuses on enhancing and promoting the student’s extra-curricular talents. It involves activities like dance, music, sports, drama, writing, etc. While the technical wing fosters academic growth, Sanskriti inspires overall development.

Prachodhana: The Technical Wing

Prachodana, the technical wing of the Amritadhara aims to instil essential technical skills in students and inspire innovation through workshops, seminars, conferences and classroom sessions.
